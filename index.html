How to participate in the ILO?

In order to purchase the BFT Token, you must go to Unicrypt BFT ILO and hold at least 2 UNCL to reserve you spot in the Pre Sale. Connect your cold Wallet and proceed as explained on the Unicrypt BFT ILO pre sale:
✅Step 1: Make sure you are eligible for the round (Burn 2UNCL and hold 50UNCL)
✅Step 2: Open our app, and select the exchange where the market of your ILO will be initialized (e.g Pancakeswap)
✅Step 3: Connect your wallet while making sure it is on the correct blockchain BSC Network.
✅Step 4: Open the ILO you are interested in and select the amount you want to participate with, then proceed with the transaction (for round 0, see details here)
✅Step 5: Once an ILO is successful (soft cap met + end block reached, or hard cap met), a publicly callable function will then be used to initialize the markets (you can see more about Successes/Failures in here).
✅Step 6: Time to withdraw your tokens from the ILO page! 🎉.

What is a BeFiT Token (BFT)?

The BFT Token, is a governance token , design to sustain the BeFiT DeFi platform, allowing you to stake, farm and act on liquidity while being rewarded.

When and how shall I receive purchased tokens?

The purchase Tokens bought during the ILO will be distributed once the ILO ends, with soft cap reached.

What is the minimum token purchase amount?

There is no minimum purchase amount required, however there is a maximum of 8 BNB

When should I send my contribution?

As soon as possible after sending your 2 UNCL. If your contribution is not sent at the latest 10 minutes before round 1, your 2 UNCL will still be burnt and you will lose your slot reservation.

What happens if the ILO fails?

You will be able to withdraw your original contribution (BNB) and 75% of your 2 UNCL, i.e. 1.5 UNCL (0.5 UNCL will still be burnt) within 1 single transaction. The reason for 25% burn on failed ILO is explained in our medium article from September 2021

ILO – Successes and Failures ?

💯 Success Scenario 1: The soft cap of the ILO is met, and the end block of the second round is reached. Scenario 2: The hard cap of the ILO is met, prior to the end block of the second round. ⏩ Publicly callable function to initialize the markets and lock the liquidity ⏩ Only then, participants can withdraw their tokens from the Dapp. ❌ Failure ​Scenario 1: The soft cap of the ILO is not met by the end block of the second round. ​Scenario 2: The markets cannot initiate due to errors in token’s smart contract . ​Scenario 3: A liquidity pool is created manually during the ILO. ​ ⏩ Participants can withdraw (ETH, xDAi, BNB…) from the Dapp.

When and on which exchanges will the BFT token be listed?

The BFT Token , will be listed on the BeFiT Platform as well as Pancakeswap, as soon as the ILO is over. The 70% locked for the liquidity will ensure its tokenomy and encourage people to invest in the Pools

No only with BNB
